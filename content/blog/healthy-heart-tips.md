Taking care of your heart does not always require major changes. Small daily habits can make a meaningful difference over time.

## 1. Stay physically active

Aim for regular movement such as walking, stretching, or light exercise. Consistency matters more than intensity in the beginning.

## 2. Eat balanced meals

Choose meals with vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and healthy protein sources. Try to reduce excess salt, sugar, and processed foods.

## 3. Monitor your blood pressure

Regular blood pressure checks can help identify early warning signs before complications develop.

## 4. Sleep well

Poor sleep can affect blood pressure, stress, and overall cardiovascular health. Try to maintain a regular sleep routine.

## 5. Schedule routine checkups

A preventive consultation can help detect hidden issues early and guide you toward healthier long-term habits.
