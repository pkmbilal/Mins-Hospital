Regular wellness checkups help identify health concerns early and support preventive care.

## Why it matters

Routine consultations, screenings, and early guidance can improve long-term health outcomes.

## What to expect

A wellness visit may include discussion of symptoms, medical history, and preventive screening recommendations.
